About Us

Hola Amigos! It's great to see you guys here, A lil introduction about us.
Our website is basically a platform which is a boon for the students of the Computer Science and Business Systems branch of the university.
We are providing the students with different resources like previous year papers, well completed and written notes, recommended books and sites and other resources, all culminated together at a place and even give the students the option to upload their own resources for the help of other fellow students.
A page to connect to the required person for any of their doubts and QR code scanner for entry into the library. Teachers can upload their assignments. deadlines, some resources they recommend, class links and time table and these will be displayed to the students on their portal.
